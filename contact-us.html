FAQ

What services do you provide?

At Inola Tech, we’re all about creativity and innovation. Our design services include web design, UI/UX design, mobile app design, branding, and eCommerce design. We also offer development services such as software development, CMS, back-end, front-end, and web development. In addition, our marketing services cover everything from SEO and content marketing to pitch deck design and conversion rate optimization.

What are the benefits of digital agencies over freelancers?

Digital agencies offer several advantages over freelance developers/designers. They typically have a team of professionals with diverse skill sets, specialized expertise, and access to resources that may not be available to individual freelancers. They also tend to have more formal processes and procedures in place to ensure that projects are completed on time and to the desired quality. Besides, digital agencies can scale their resources up or down depending on the size and complexity of your project, making them a more versatile option for businesses of all sizes.

Do you have examples of your projects?

We believe that portfolio is a testament to the quality of work and the results our team achieves for each and every client. You can browse the Inola Tech website, Behance, Dribbble, or Webflow to see examples of Inola Tech’s projects, including websites, mobile apps, branding cases, and more.

Can you provide services to early-stage startups?

Our digital company has extensive experience working with early-stage startups, and we’re committed to providing tailored solutions that address their specific needs. We understand the importance of agility and flexibility in the early stages of a startup’s journey, so our team is dedicated to bringing the client’s vision to life as fast and efficiently as possible.

Do you offer SEO services?

Yes, Inola Tech offers comprehensive SEO services to help clients improve their online visibility and drive organic traffic to their websites. Our team of experts is well-versed in the latest SEO techniques and strategies and is committed to staying up-to-date with industry trends and best practices. We offer a range of SEO services, including keyword research, on-page optimization, and off-page optimization, along with analytics and reporting.

Why don’t you provide a fixed price?

Our team believes that every project is unique and requires a customized approach, which is why Inola Tech doesn’t have a strict price list. Instead, we strive to provide transparent and fair pricing for each client based on the project’s requirements and complexity, business goals, and special requests.

How can I be sure that I will get my design in time?

We understand the importance of timely delivery and are committed to meeting our client’s deadlines. For this purpose, Inola Tech has a proven track record of delivering high-quality designs on time, using project management tools to ensure that our team stays on track. Additionally, we provide regular updates and communicate proactively with our clients to ensure that everyone is on the same page.
